What you walk out with

You build one of these for yourself in the next hour, and put it on a public URL. No coding.

OpenAlex and ORCID

  • OpenAlex, a free and open catalog of the world's papers citations, co-authors, and a year-by-year citation count since 2012
  • ORCID, short for Open Researcher and Contributor ID a free 16-digit ID that distinguishes you from every other researcher
  • Do not remember your ORCID? Ask Claude to look it up for you

How this hour works

You will add a second repo, MyPublications, next to your ClaudeLab in ws.

  • Gather your papers first, and check they are yours OpenAlex is not error-free, and Claude can make mistakes
  • Then interview → spec → demo to build the page, like Lesson 1
  • All you have to do is explain your goal when unsure, have Claude ask you
  • Commit each step, publish at the end

You should already have your ClaudeLab in the ws folder, be signed in to GitHub, and have the Code tab open.

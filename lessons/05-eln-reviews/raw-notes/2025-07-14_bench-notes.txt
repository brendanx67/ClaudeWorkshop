tb-digest notes 7/14   (LP)

samples: TB-0417, TB-0419, TB-0420   (0418 tube cracked in the rack, tossed it)
plasma digest for the tf-scan runs. following the usual SDC protocol off the lab wiki.

- pellets outta the -80, ~20 ug each (BCA from fri, numbers in slack). resusp each in 50 uL
  100mM TEAB + 1% SDC. vortex, quick spin.
     -> want 200 ng/uL for the run
- reduce: 10 mM DTT, 56C, 30 min. heat block was reading 54-55, wouldn't quite hit 56. close enough
- alkylate: 20 mM IAA, RT in the dark, 30 min
- LysC first, 1:100, 37C ~2h
- then trypsin. BUT -- outta the sequencing-grade aliquots. grabbed a tube from the -20 door,
  label half smudged, pretty sure it's the promega lot, exp stamp looks like 11/2024. added 1:50.
- digest o/n at 37C. started ~6:15pm. came in late tues, pulled them ~10:30am, so call it 16h-ish,
  maybe a bit more
- quench + crash the SDC: FA to ~1%, spun 10 min, took the sup
- C18 stagetip cleanup. 2x wash (protocol says 3x but I was almost out of wash buffer and had a
  seminar to get to). eluted 50% ACN.
- speedvac to dry. resusp in 20 uL 0.1% FA.
- QC inject: 1:10, 2 uL on column

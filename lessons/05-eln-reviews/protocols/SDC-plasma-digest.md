# SOP: SDC plasma tryptic digest (tf-scan atlas)

Standard operating procedure. Any departure from these steps must be recorded in the notebook
entry's **Deviations** section.

## Input
- 20 µg protein per sample (BCA-quantified).

## Lysis / resuspension
- Resuspend each pellet in 100 mM TEAB + 1% SDC.

## Reduction
- 10 mM DTT, **56 °C**, 30 min.

## Alkylation
- 20 mM IAA, room temperature, in the dark, 30 min.

## Digestion
- LysC 1:100, 37 °C, 2 h.
- Then **sequencing-grade** trypsin, 1:50, 37 °C, **overnight (12–18 h)**.
- Use sequencing-grade trypsin within its expiry date. **Do not substitute expired, mislabeled,
  or non-sequencing-grade lots** without recording it as a deviation.

## Quench & SDC removal
- Acidify with FA to ~1%, centrifuge 10 min, retain supernatant.

## Desalting
- C18 StageTip, **3× wash**, elute in 50% ACN.

## Final
- Dry (SpeedVac), resuspend in 0.1% FA to **200 ng/µL** for injection.

## Records required
- Lot number and expiry for every enzyme.
- Exact sample IDs processed.
- Any deviation from the above, with reason.

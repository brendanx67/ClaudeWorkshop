The two mornings

Critical lesson

What you do is explain.

Claude is a remarkable tool — but it needs you to explain your work. We will teach you to record critical explanations as skills and commands that keep you from re-explaining.

Train Claude like a talented new-hire

  • Start with small projects.
  • Capture what you teach.
  • Build on success.

Claude will never be perfect. Keep training.
